      About Assyrian Villages

      Our Mission

      Assyrian Villages is committed to documenting and preserving the history of Assyrian communities worldwide. We gather historical photographs, research village backgrounds, and trace the origins and movements of families and tribes. The goal is to create a lasting and accessible record of who we are and where we come from.

      Why It's Important

      Assyrian history is disappearing. Villages have been emptied. Stories have gone untold. Without action, entire parts of our identity will be lost. Preserving this history is not just about honoring the past. It is about knowing who we are today and protecting the roots of our culture. These villages and their histories connect us. They carry the legacy of our strength, our traditions, and our survival.

      Our Vision

      Our goal is to build the most complete and accessible digital archive of Assyrian village history. This platform will serve as a trusted resource for learning, connection, and preservation. It will help Assyrians around the world rediscover their roots, educate others, and keep our history alive for generations to come.
